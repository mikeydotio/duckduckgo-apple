A spotlight-style overlay triggered by ⌘K (or a toolbar button). Type to filter past chats or jump straight into a new one. Keyboard-first, fast, no sidebar needed. Dismisses on Escape or selection.

C — Address bar suggestions

When the address bar is focused, recent Duck.ai chats surface as a distinct group in the suggestions dropdown (alongside bookmarks/history). Typing "duck.ai" or "?" narrows to AI results only. Selecting one resumes the chat.
